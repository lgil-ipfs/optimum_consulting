I can fill this role for you and help you reach your full potential.

My Approach

A collaborative relationship built on trust.

I approach each client in a non-judgmental manner, being sensitive to the circumstances of their issues, and empathic to their experiences. Clients can expect to work within a collaborative, goal-oriented relationship.

  • Clarify the primary issues to be dealt with
  • Set concrete and attainable goals
  • Appraise your progress regularly

About Dr. John Ogrodniczuk

I am a Professor and Director of the Psychotherapy Program in the Department of Psychiatry, University of British Columbia. In my role as Director, I oversee the operations of one of North America’s largest medically based psychotherapy training programs.

My research has been supported by various provincial and national funding bodies, and has led to more than 250 publications. I am also the Founder of HeadsUpGuys, a leading global resource for men with depression.
